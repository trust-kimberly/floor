Our Story

Since 1992, Floor Sanding of Mass, Inc. has been one of the most successful hardwood floor sanders in the New England area. We are committed to the highest standards of workmanship and our professionalism and customer service remain our highest priorities. In addition, we are proud members of the National Wood Flooring Association (NWFA). Several of our employees have attended seminars and courses organized by NWFA.

We are a small company that is growing rapidly due to our extremely high level of client satisfaction. Currently, we have approximately a dozen full-time employees, each with several years of hardwood floor experience. In order to handle jobs quickly and efficiently, we operate with a fleet of eight teams. Each team includes skilled craftsmen who take pride in their work. Our clients include both residential and commercial properties.

At Floor Sanding of Mass, Inc. our excellent reputation stems from our commitment to providing the highest level of personal service, trained trustworthy staff, and quick response time.
